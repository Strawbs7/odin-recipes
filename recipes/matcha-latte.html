Matcha Latte

A Matcha latte is a soothing and vibrant beverage that combines the earthy richness of matcha with the creaminess of steamed milk Envision a beverage that embodies both the invigorating essence of finely ground green tea and the comforting embrace of frothy, velvety milk. A Matcha latte is a symphony of flavors and textures, crafted to delight the senses and provide a moment of serene indulgence.

Ingredients

  • Matcha, also known as powdered green tea or green tea powder
  • Hot water, about 176ºF or 80ºC
  • Soy milk
  • Sugar (optional)

Steps

  1. Sift the matcha powder into a bowl or cup. This will minimize any lumps and yield a smooth latte.
  2. Add the hot water to the bowl.
  3. Whisk briskly, drawing a letter W, not the usual circular pattern. Blend the hot water and matcha powder until no lumps remain. The matcha should be smooth and a little foamy.
  4. Steam the cold milk with a steaming wand or froth the warmed milk with a handheld milk frother.
  5. Pour the milk into the matcha. Sprinkle additional matcha on top.
